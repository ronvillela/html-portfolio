The Best Movies According to Ron

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather Trilogy

This is my top most favorite movie of all time.
The age old themes of honor and family and the use of the characters and dialogue to relay these themes are excellent.
Consquently, the movie has won multiple awards and is still viewed by many today.

Limitless

This movie's plot based on a man down on his luck and willing to try anything to become a success is very appealing.
In the end he understands that he must control this new power before it takes contorl of him.

Goodwill Hunting

I connect with the characters and the plot of this money so much.
The main character, Will, reminds me of growing in one of the toughest neighborhoods in Boston.
While dealing with the daily drama of a poor kid from the hood, Will eventually comes to terms with his demons and makes progress towards becoming a man.
